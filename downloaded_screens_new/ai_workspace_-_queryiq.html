Analyzing `transaction_ledger` joined with `loyalty_programs`. I've constructed a query to aggregate mean transaction amounts grouped by tier, filtered for the previous fiscal year.

SQL • snowflake 
SELECT 
    l.loyalty_tier,
    ROUND(AVG(t.amount), 2) as avg_transaction_value,
    COUNT(t.transaction_id) as volume
FROM transaction_ledger t
JOIN customer_loyalty l ON t.user_id = l.user_id
WHERE t.timestamp >= DATEADD(month, -12, CURRENT_DATE())
GROUP BY 1
ORDER BY 2 DESC;
